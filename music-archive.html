Here Comes a Thought (Unfinished Retro Remix)

A remix of Steven Universe's Here Comes a Thought. I wanted to go all out on this and use as much cool shit as possible to make it. This version only has my Gameboy, a floppydrive, some N64 samples and some SNES samples in it. I was going to use a Ti-84 calculator, an Atari 2600, and a PC buzzer for it but I never ended up finishing it.

Crate-Runner OST

Looking back on it, this was probably the first game I ever really released. I created it as a final project for my A.P. Computer Science project over the course of a week. It follows the story of one proto-dudebro working at Blamazoon. And by follows the story of one proto-dudebro working at Blamazoon, I mean that there's a dude walking on a conveyor belt jumping over crates. Gotta have that player manual lore, right?
